About Us

Discover the rich legacy of SIWS College .

S.I.W.S. Degree College in Commerce and Economics was established in 1980 and Smt. Thirumalai College of Science in 1990. The Vision of SIWS Colleges is to continually strive to respond to realities and social changes through knowledge empowerment. This is achieved through the Motto of SIWS ‘Vidya Dhanam Sarva Dhanat Pradhanam’. In pursuance of its Vision, SIWS is dedicated to produce socially responsible and intellectually capable citizens of India.
