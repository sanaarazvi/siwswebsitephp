Library

Dr Kishore Manilal Shah Library of SIWS College is an integral part of academic and research work at the college. The library provides information services to support the teaching, learning, research and outreach activities of the Institute.The library is housed on the 4th floor of the Main building. In a total layout area of 4000sq.ft, there is a Reading hall with a capacity to accommodate 200 students and 20 teachers, a Research Center which offers services to research students.The Library has a collection of 15000 print books and subscribes to about 55 print periodicals pertaining to various programs and subjects offered by the institute every year.

Turf/Basketball area

SIWS College has a well-maintained outdoor turf used for football, cricket, and other outdoor sports. The turf is primarily synthetic or grass-covered and suitable for college-level sports activities and tournaments. It is also occasionally used for sports events, cultural programs, and outdoor gatherings. Approximate area: The turf is of standard college size, roughly half to full football field size, enough for multiple sports activities simultaneously.The college has a dedicated basketball court within the campus. The court is outdoor, marked with standard dimensions (approx. 28 m × 15 m) and has proper hoops and backboards.

Cafeteria

Situated in the rear of the college main building, a suitable place for grapevine communication; serving folks with aromatic beverages and appetizing brunch. The institute pertains its tradition not only through the infrastructure, academics, staffs and students but also in the healthy and hygienic food served by the canteen. The Canteen also serves delicious and sumptuous South Indian varieties including sweets at various ceremonies.
